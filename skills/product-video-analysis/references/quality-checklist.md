# Quality Checklist

## Coverage

- [ ] All 25 modules from `full-analysis-standard.md` are present.
- [ ] Added animation detail did not replace macro strategy, design, implementation, or exercises.
- [ ] The entire duration is covered by the timeline.
- [ ] Repeated patterns are grouped only after at least one full example is analyzed.

## Evidence

- [ ] Product facts are verified with official sources or explicitly unverified.
- [ ] Observations are supported by frames, audio, metadata, or transcript.
- [ ] Timing, frame counts, BPM, and curves are labeled Estimated.
- [ ] Creative intent, original software, and layer structure are labeled Inferred.
- [ ] Fast motion uses multi-frame evidence.

## Animation detail

- [ ] Every major motion has a common name.
- [ ] Properties and start/end states are described.
- [ ] Duration in seconds and frames is estimated.
- [ ] Easing family and graph character are described.
- [ ] Overshoot, bounce, anticipation, overlap, and settle are distinguished correctly.
- [ ] Sound sync points are documented.
- [ ] Product relevance is stated.

## Strategy

- [ ] Product truth leads to communication objective.
- [ ] Creative proposition is one sentence.
- [ ] Copy, image, motion, and sound roles are differentiated.
- [ ] Feature order reflects audience comprehension, not merely UI navigation.
- [ ] The brand reveal and close have a clear reason.

## Learning value

- [ ] Recommended implementation is actionable.
- [ ] Original-technique hypotheses are separated from recreation advice.
- [ ] A simplified version is provided.
- [ ] Exercises have outputs and evaluation criteria.
- [ ] Transferable rules are extracted.
- [ ] The method is applied to the user's product.

## PDF

- [ ] Every page has been rendered to an image.
- [ ] No clipping, overflow, missing glyphs, blank pages, or duplicated pages.
- [ ] Screenshots remain legible and include timecodes.
- [ ] Tables fit the page and do not use unreadably small type.
- [ ] Links and references work.
