# Sound Design Framework

Sound is part of the product argument. Analyze it at the same time resolution as motion and copy.

## Layer model

1. **Music bed** — tempo, harmony, energy curve, genre, tonal identity.
2. **Rhythmic accents** — percussion or pulses that organize edits.
3. **Transition effects** — whooshes, risers, downlifters, reverse swells.
4. **Object impacts** — thumps, taps, ticks, snaps, soft hits.
5. **UI effects** — clicks, toggles, typing, confirmations, notifications.
6. **Material texture** — paper, glass, metal, digital noise, room tone.
7. **Voice-over / generated voice** — semantic carrier and trust signal.
8. **Silence / subtraction** — creates anticipation, seriousness, and focus.

## Motion-to-sound mapping

| Visual event | Sound family | Product meaning |
|---|---|---|
| Scale pop | short click + soft low impact | Immediate response with weight |
| Back-out overshoot | impact at peak or target, not both by default | Confident activation |
| Fast slide/push | narrow whoosh matched to direction | Speed and continuity |
| Camera push-in | subtle riser or filtered swell | Focus and importance |
| Hard cut on beat | transient or percussion hit | Precision |
| Light sweep | high-frequency shimmer | Finish and material polish |
| Text scramble | granular ticks/noise | Computation, resolution |
| UI state change | tactile click | Control and causality |
| Large reveal | low impact + harmonic lift | Scale and significance |
| Calm brand hold | music tail, room tone, reduced effects | Trust and confidence |

## Product-character palettes

### Speed

- Short attack, tight transients, minimal reverberation
- Faster edit pulse
- Directional whooshes used sparingly

### Precision and control

- Dry clicks, restrained low impacts
- Clear separation between event sounds
- Little or no comedic bounce sound

### Creativity and exploration

- Layered tonal ticks, evolving textures, unexpected but coherent timbres
- More call-and-response between motion and music

### Trust and finance

- Stable low-frequency foundation
- Controlled dynamics
- Avoid toy-like boings, excessive glitch, or chaotic high frequencies unless contrast is intentional

### Calm intelligence

- Soft attack, longer tails, reduced event density
- Voice remains intelligible above the bed
- Silence around key statements

## Caution

A visible motion does not prove a dedicated sound effect exists; it may be carried by the music. Use **Observed** only when the effect is audible and separable. Otherwise label it **Inferred** or describe it as a recommended recreation.
